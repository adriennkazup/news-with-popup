A summer of Clásicos

Real Madrid are going through a thrilling summer. The Champions League and LaLiga have some challenging ties ahead of them. Three of those are against Barcelona. Fans are going to enjoy three Clásicos throughout July and August. First, they go head-to-head in the International Champions Cup and after, they face off in the Spanish Super Cup.

Presenting Real Madrid's 2017-18 LaLiga schedule

Presenting Real Madrid's 2017-18 LaLiga schedule

The Whites will face Deportivo de La Coruña at Riazor in their first game on the weekend of the 19th and 20th August. They will play Atlético on matchday 12 (the weekend of November 19th) and will host Barcelona on matchday 17 (20th December). Real Madrid now know the LaLiga schedule for 2017-18, which will start on the weekend of 19 and 20 August. The champions The Whites will face Deportivo de La Coruña at Riazor in their first game.

The derbies against Atletico Madrid will be on matchday 12 (the weekend of November 19th) at the Wanda Metropolitano and matchday 31, either the 7th or 8th April, at the Santiago Bernabéu. The Whites’ stadium will host the first Clasico on matchday 17, which will be played on Wednesday, December 20th. The return tie will be played on matchday 36, on the weekend of May 6th. The Whites will close the LaLiga season at home to Villarreal, the weekend of May 20th.
